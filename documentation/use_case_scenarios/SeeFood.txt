See Food 

Context: 
Cook is logged in 

Primary Flow 
    1. Cook request to view available food 
    2. System checks for all food items in the system 
    3. System presents cook with a list of all food in the system, presented in largest to smallest quantity 
    4. Cook views list of food 

Alternative Flows 

System has no food 

A2. Display alert that there is no food in inventory 
A3. The cook acknowledges that there is no food. 
A4. Return to primary flow step 1.
